Recreating the table resets current rows.
Danger zone
Irreversible actions. Be careful.
Tips:
  • Resize columns by dragging the handle between headers (cursor changes). Double-click the handle to auto-fit to the widest cell.
  • Blank rows are skipped automatically.
  • Paste from Excel/Sheets: copy cells and paste anywhere in the table to add rows.
  • To delete a specific row, use the Delete button on that row.
